2024 Gardening

This year we needed to beef up the fences around our gardens to help keep out the rabbits, and a wily dog who likes to eat our produce.

We also ended up planting a third apple tree to continue to expand our mini orchard.

Planting plan for this year:

  • Plot One: Lettuce, Brussels, Cabbage and Potatoes
  • Plot Two: Onions, Dragons Tongue Beans, a few pea plants
  • Plot Three:Corn, Sweet Potato
  • Plot Four: Black Beans
  • Plot Five: Peppers, and accidental lettuce
