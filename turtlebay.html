Turtle Bay Utilities Exploration

This is a four station interactive that I created during my time at Ideum for the City of Redding and the Turtle Bay Exploration Park.

A full portfolio video can be found on Ideums Website

It consisted of an ArcGIS map explorer that visitors could use to learn about the diffenent utilites in the area, as well as important landmarks

There were also a few different interactive scenes to help visitors learn about how things they may do every day might effect the local community.
