Qualcomm X-Prize Exhibit

This was a four person, three station multi-user interactive funded by Qualcomm aimed to educate and inspire about the future of medicine.

It consisted of a station to optimize a medical scanning device for specific scenarios, a challenge between two visitors to diagnose a patient and a station where visistors could scan their own temperature, galvonic skin response, and get a heart rate reading.

This exhibit was five tables spread across five different science centers across the United States.
